About Me

Hey. I'm Dani. I am a London based theatremaker. I trained at Ecole Jacques Lecoq, Soho Writers' Lab and the University of Essex. I am currently touring my solo play Cadets.

Theatre

I've performed with... National Theatre Young Studio, Punchdrunk, Camden People's Theatre, Siobhan Davies Studios, DanceXchange, Mercury Theatre, Gideon Reeling and Duckie.

I've written plays for...Camden People's Theatre, Battersea Arts Centre (Wandsworth Arts Fringe), Roundhouse Theatre, Ensemble Studio Theater (New York), Lakeside Theatre, and Cambridge Junction.

Awards

I was awarded a scholarship to attend New York Theatre Intensives (2013) - a six week playwriting and acting programme.
